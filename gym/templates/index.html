Zumba Fitness

Zumba Fitness is a lively dance-based workout that combines infectious music with exhilarating choreography. Participants will enjoy a full-body workout while dancing to various rhythms, including salsa, merengue, and hip-hop. This class is perfect for those looking to have fun while burning calories, boosting mood, and improving cardiovascular health..

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

Our HIIT class delivers a fast-paced workout that alternates between intense bursts of exercise and short recovery periods. This efficient training method helps participants burn calories, build endurance, and improve cardiovascular fitness in a fun and energetic environment. Classes are adaptable to all fitness levels, ensuring everyone can push their limits and achieve their goals.

Yoga Flow

The Yoga Flow class combines dynamic movements and breathwork to enhance flexibility, balance, and relaxation. Suitable for all levels, participants will explore various asanas (poses) while fostering mindfulness and body awareness. This calming practice is designed to reduce stress, improve mental clarity, and promote overall well-being.

Strength Training

In our Strength Training class, members focus on building muscle and increasing overall strength through resistance exercises. Each session incorporates free weights, machines, and bodyweight movements to target major muscle groups. Led by experienced trainers, this class emphasizes proper form and technique, helping participants maximize their results and prevent injury..
